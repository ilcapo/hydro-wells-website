There are several signs that indicate your well or pump may need attention:

  • Low water pressure: Weak flow can indicate pump problems or a drop in water level.
  • Strange taste, odor or color of water: This can be a sign of contamination or system problems.
  • Strange pump noises: Clicking, humming or constant noises indicate the pump is working harder than normal.
  • Increase in electricity bill: An inefficiently working pump consumes more energy.

Notice any of these symptoms? Our team can quickly diagnose the problem with specialized equipment. Schedule an evaluation.
